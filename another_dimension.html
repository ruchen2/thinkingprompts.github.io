Another Dimension

  • Difficulties involved in moving or relocating an object along a line are removed if the object acquires the ability to move in two dimensions (along a plane). Accordingly, problems connected with movement or relocation of an object on one plane are removed by switching to a three-dimensional space
  • Use a multi-story arrangement of objects instead of a single-story arrangement. Use a multi-layered assembly of objects instead of a single layer
  • Tilt or re-orient the object, lay it on its side
  • Use another side of a given area
  • Use optical lines falling onto neighboring areas or onto the reverse side of the area available
