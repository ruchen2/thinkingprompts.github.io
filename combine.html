SCAMPER: Combine

  • Can we combine functions?
  • Can we combine features?
  • What components in the system could be combined?
  • Can we combine different materials: such as mixtures, blends, composites and coatings?
  • Can we add more functions?
  • Can we add more features?

Excercise: Combine random objects

  • Select twenty objects at random (e.g, home, work, street, museum, store)
  • Make two lists of ten objects each on the left and right sides of the paper
  • Pick an object from the left and combine it with on the right
  • When you find a promising new combination, refine and elaborate it into a new invention
