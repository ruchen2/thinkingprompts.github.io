Critical Thinking Skills

  • Interpretation (clarify meaning, categorize, decode significance)
  • Analysis (examine ideas, identify arguments, identify reasons and claims)
  • Inference (query evidence, conjecture alternatives, draw conclusions)
  • Evaluation (assess credibility of claims, assess quality of arguments)
  • Explanation (state results, justify procedures, present arguments)
  • Self-Regulatioin (self-monitor, self-correct)
