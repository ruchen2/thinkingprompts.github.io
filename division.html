Division

  1. List internal components of the challenge.
  2. Divide the challenge in one of three ways:
    • Take a component and rearrange its location or timing.
    • Cut a component along any physical line and rearrange it.
    • Divide the challenge into smaller pieces, which still possess all the characteristics of the whole.
  3. Imagine the resulting concept.
  4. Ask questions:
    • What are the potential benefits?
    • Who would want the new idea?
    • If you are trying to solve a specific problem, how can the idea help address that particular challenge?
