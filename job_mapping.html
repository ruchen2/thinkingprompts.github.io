Job Mapping

  • Determine the JTBD
  • Define the cognitvie portion of the JTBD: objective, planning, & identifying resources
  • Locate the enablers for achieving the JTBD: all items and info that is needed to get the job done
  • Prepare all located enablers for achieving the JTBD: activities preparing the inputs to execute the JTBD
  • Confirm that all preparation are complete
  • Execute the JTBD
  • Monitor the experience
  • Modify the experience: either in real time or in future execution of the same JTBD
  • Conclude the job
  • Identify opportunities for innovation
