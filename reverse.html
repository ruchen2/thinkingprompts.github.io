SCAMPER: Reverse

  • Reverse direction: sequence, arrangement, position, procedure, movement and rotation?
  • Reverse size: big/small, high/low, wide/narrow, more/less and long/short?
  • What are the opposites?
  • Reverse roles?
  • Can it be reverse, up/down, top/bottom, left/right, front/back?
  • Can it be reverse, in/out?
  • Consider it backward?
  • Do the unexpected?
  • Reverse cause and effect?
  • Reverse assumptions?
  • Reverse perspectives (e.g.,male/female, friend/enemy)?
  • Opposite of common senses?
  • Opposite of popular views?
  • Reverse to the basics of the challenge and rephrase the challenge in a more general way?
  • Reverse to the origin of the challenge and rephrase the challenge in a broader sense?

Rearrange

  • What other arrangement might be better?
  • Interchange components? Other pattern? Other layout?
  • Other sequence? Other order?
  • Change pace? Change schedule?

Think Paradoxically

  • Convert the problem into a paradox. Ask "What is the opposite or contradiction of the problem". Then imagine both existing at the same time
  • Summarize the paradox into a booktitle that capture the essence and paradox of the problem. Should be two words, an adjective and a noun
  • Find an analog that reflects the essence of the paradox
  • What is the unique feature or activity of the analogy
  • Use an equivalent of the unique feature to trigger new ideas
  • Build into a new idea
