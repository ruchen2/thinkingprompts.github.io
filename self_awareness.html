Develop High Self-Awareness

  • Examine how you make appraisals
    • Appraisals: impressions, interpretations, evaluations, and expectations about people and situations
    • Influencing factors: background, experience, natural abilities, and beliefs
    • Forms: thoughts and inner dialogues
  • Tune into your senses
    • Senses: seeing, hearing, smelling, tasting, and touching
    • Senses: sources of your data about the world
    • Distinguish between sensory data from appraisals
  • Get in touch with your feelings
    • Spontaneous emotional responses to your interpretations and expectations
    • Six basic emotions: happiness, anger, sadness, fear, disgust, and surprise
    • Wheel of emotions: joy/sadness, anger/fear, trust/disgust, surprise/anticipation
    • Become attuned to corresponding physical and behaviour manifestations
  • Learn what your intentions are
    • Immediate desires and long-term desires
    • Apparent intention and hidden agenda
  • Pay attention to your actions
    • Monitor your actions
    • Observe the impact of your actions
    • Regonize different ways people respond to your actions
