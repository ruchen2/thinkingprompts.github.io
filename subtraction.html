Subtraction

  1. List internal components of the challenge.
  2. Select an essential component and remove it
    • The component is removed entirely.
    • One function or feature of the component is removed.
  3. Imagine the resulting concept.
  4. Replace the removed component with an internal or external component.
  5. Ask questions:
    • What are the potential benefits?
    • Who would want the new idea?
    • If you are trying to solve a specific problem, how can the idea help address that particular challenge?
