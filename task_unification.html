Task Unification

  1. List internal and external components of the challenge.
  2. Select one component and assign it an additional task.
    • Make an external component perform a task that the challenge accomplished already.
    • Make an internal component do something new or extra.
    • Make an internal component perform the function of an external component.
  3. Imagine the resulting concept.
  4. Ask questions:
    • What are the potential benefits?
    • Who would want the new idea?
    • If you are trying to solve a specific problem, how can the idea help address that particular challenge?
