Make Decisions Using Two Systems

  • System-1 Thinking
    • Relies heavily on situational cues, salient memories, and heuristic thinking to arrive quickly and confidently at judgments, particularly when situations are familiar and immediate action is required
    • Reactive
    • Instinctive
    • Quick
    • Holistic
  • System-2 Thinking
    • Is useful for judgments in unfamiliar situations, for processing abstract concepts, and for deliberating when there is time for planning and more comprehensive consideration
    • Reflective
    • Deliberative
    • Analytical
    • Procedural
