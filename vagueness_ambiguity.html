Resolve Problematic Vagueness and Ambiguity

  • Contextualize
    • Who said it to whom?
    • When and where was it said?
    • What was the topic of the conversation?
    • Was the comment meant to be ironic, hyperbolic, misleading, or deceptive?
    • Were technical terms, abbreviations, symbols, slangs, code words, double entendre, euphemisms, or acronyms used?
  • Clarify original intent
  • Negotiate the meaning
  • Use qualifications, exceptions, or exclusions
  • Stipulate the meaning
